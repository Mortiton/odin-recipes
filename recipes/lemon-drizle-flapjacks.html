Lemon Drizzle Flapjacks

Description

Lemon oat bars packed with lemon zest, lemon extract and a combination of lemon juice and icing sugar drizzled in top.

Ingredients

  • Porridge oats
  • Unsalted butter
  • Golden syrup
  • Light brown soft sugar
  • Lemon zest
  • Lemon juice
  • Icing sugar

Steps

    Preheat the oven

  1. Preheat the oven to 150Â°C

    2. Prepare wet ingredients

  2. Using a medium sized saucepan, combine the unsalted button, 1 tbsp lemon juice and light brown sugar
  3. Heat gently, over a low to medium heat, stirring until melted
  4. Set aside to cool slightly

    5. Mix with oats

  5. Place porridge oats in a large mixing bowl
  6. Add lemon zest
  7. Add the melted butter mixture
  8. Mix well until all the oats are covered

    9. Line and press

  9. Line a 20cm (8 inches) square baking tin with baking paper. Leave a little overhang and lightly grease
  10. Transfer the flapjack mixture into the lined baking tin
  11. Press down firmly using a spatula or the back of a spoon

    12. Bake

  12. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes

    13. Cool in the tin

  13. Remove the flapjacks from the oven. They may appear slightly soft but golden brown in colour
  14. Allow them to harden and cool to room temperature in the tin

    15. Lift and set on rack

  15. Once cool and firm, lift the flapjacks out of the tin using the overhanging baking paper
  16. Remove the paper and place the cooled flapjack slab on a wire rack

    17. Prepare lemon glaze

  17. Mix 1.5 tbsp of lemon juice with icing suagr to create a thick yellow icing. You may need to adjust the amounts to get a drizzling consistency without it being too runny

    18. Drizzle and set

  18. Drizzle the icing mixture onto the flapjacks using a fork and allow to set

    19. Slice and store

  19. Once icing has set, use a sharp knife to slice the flapjack slab into portions
  20. Store in an airtight container. Either in single layers or mutliple using baking paper to separate the layers.
