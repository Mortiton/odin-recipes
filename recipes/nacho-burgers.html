Nacho Burgers

Nacho Beef Burgers

Description

Serves: 4

Restraurant quality burgers with melted cheese, burger sauace, quacamole and tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Salt and pepper
  • American cheese slices
  • Jalapeno burger sauce
  • Guacamole
  • Nachos
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Burger buns

Steps

    Preheat the grill or grilling pan

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop grilling pan over a medium heat

    2. Season and mix beef mince

  2. Place beef mince in a large bowl
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Gently mix together

    5. Divide and shape the patties

  5. Divide the seasoned beef into four patties
  6. Shape the patties into loose balls

    7. Cook the patties

  7. Place the beef balls into the pre-heated pan
  8. Using a square of baking paper, cover each ball and press into a pattie using a spatula
  9. Press your thumb into the middle of each pattie then remove the paper
  10. Cook the patties on the grill for 3-4 minutes on each side or until done to desired level

    11. Toast the burger buns

  11. While the patties are cooking, splut the burger buns and toast them on the grill until golden brown

    12. Melt the cheese

  12. In the last minute of cooking place a slice of cheese ontop of top of each pattie
  13. Turn off the head and cover the pan.This allows the cheese to melt and the burgers to rest

    14. Assemble the nacho burgers

  14. Spread a tablespoon of jalapeno burger sauce onto the top of the bottom bun
  15. Place a cheese covered pattie on top of the sauce
  16. Spoon guacamole and sprinkle crushed nachos on top of the patties
  17. Add a handful of shredded lettuce
  18. Place the top half of the burger bun on top
